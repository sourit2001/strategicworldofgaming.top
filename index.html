About 1v1.LOL

1v1.LOL is an exciting battle royale game that combines building mechanics with intense shooting action. Build your fortress, collect weapons, and compete against players worldwide in this fast-paced combat game.

Game Features

• Build and shoot mechanics similar to Fortnite

• Multiple game modes including 1v1, Box Fights, and Battle Royale

• Cross-platform gameplay

• Free-to-play with regular updates

How to Play

Use WASD to move, mouse to aim and shoot. Press Q to enter building mode, and use your mouse to place building pieces. Master both building and shooting to become the ultimate champion!

About Geometry Vibes

Geometry Vibes is a reaction-based arcade game where you avoid obstacles, traps and spikes that come at you in waves. Your goal in the game is to keep your arrow on the route and take it as far as possible. Geometry Vibes, where the excitement never ends, invites you to challenge.

Game Features

  • Simple gameplay with challenging mechanics
  • Multiple game modes: Classic, Endless, and Race Mode
  • 2, 3, and 4 player support for multiplayer fun
  • Relaxing music and reactions
  • Ability to purchase new arrow skins, tails and colors

How to Play

  1. Click "MOUSE LEFT-CLICK" or "UP ARROW" and hold to fly up
  2. Release the button to dive down
  3. Try to continue without hitting obstacles
  4. In multiplayer modes, players are controlled with "UP ARROW", "H" and "L"

The game is simple to play but difficult to master. Focus on timing, rhythm, and anticipation to improve your skills!

Game Modes

Classic Mode: 10 levels with increasing difficulty. When you reach level 10, the game becomes extremely challenging.

Endless Mode: No limit mode where you push yourself to improve your record. Speed increases with distance.

Race Mode: Challenge your friends in multiplayer mode with 2, 3, or 4 players on the same keyboard.
